Dear Shayan,

There are souls the world is simply softer for holding, and you are one of them. Kindness seems to arrive in a room a breath before you do, and stays long after you have gone.

I have watched you stay gentle on days that would have hardened anyone else. So let this be a letter, written in light, for a heart as rare and as loved as yours.

a rose, that never wilts

When they try to dim you

Let the world say what it will. They mistake your softness for weakness, never knowing it takes a quiet kind of courage to stay kind while your own body grows tired.

You have carried more than most could lift, and still you choose tenderness over bitterness. Whatever they take from you, they can never touch this: you are deeply, fiercely loved, and you always will be.

two hearts, beating as one

Should you ever forget

When the nights feel long and the world feels unkind, come back to this little corner of light and remember who you are.

You are treasured beyond measure. Not for what you can do, nor for how strong you feel today, but for the gentle, beautiful soul you simply are. You have never walked this road alone, and as long as I breathe, you never will.
